The process of processing systems with Masha Andrianova and Nat Kukina

In this special quarantine instalment of our faculty podcast BE KIND, hosted by Lesya Kuranova, Masha and Nat discuss similarities and differences in their approaches to graphic design based on the example of their final projects. The talk is centred around the topic of systematisation and organisation and their place in design, as well as how those phenomena influence the everyday life.

Guest appearance: Maria Kharmandarian.
