Tattoo and design as fields of work
with Liza Falyugina

22nd of June
19:00 (GMT+3)

This event is an interview on the topics of similarities and differences in graphic design and tattoo. How similar the business side and how different the process of making the work. Lisa is sharing her personal experience of becoming a tattoo artist and tells how design education helps her along the way.
