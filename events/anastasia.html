Dancing challenge
with Nastia Suchkova

19th of June
17:00 (GMT+3)

This event reveals the connection between Graphics world and the world of dance and movement. A group of 11 people took a part at the challenge watching the same dance moves however resulting with a different unique interpretation of words and images. In the event you will get into a playful atmosphere of dance, watch how people of different background perform, and understand how written words play role in it.

The event will be held in English.
