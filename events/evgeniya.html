Pinhole photography in metro
with Evgeniya Velichko

20th of June
17:00 (GMT+3) (GMT+3)

Evgeniya is going to share some experience with a pinhole camera. She works with photography in Moscow metro, which is quite complicated with the pinhole camera, due to lack of light, amount of people around, and the tripod use ban. Her project is about the intensity of light in different metro stations and their comparison by pinhole means. This event is a story about how the project went and how everything was made.

The event will be held in Russian.
