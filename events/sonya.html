Soviet graphical sound and synthetic music
with Sonia Solokhina

20th of June
19:00 (GMT+3)

A discussion on graphic sound and synthetic music which was created by Soviet inventors, engineers and physicists, about how Soviet inventions influenced the development of electronic music in general. Russia can rightfully be considered the birthplace of electronic music. The podcast will be accompanied by music (set) which is related to these topics. This is a small lecture with dj set — about forgotten history, about the great Soviet achievements, about their importance in culture and development, and about music.

The event will be held in English.
