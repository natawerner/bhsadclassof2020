Nonchalant Talks
with Tasha Shubina

18th of June
19:00 (GMT+3)

"Nonchalant talks" is a selection of monologues from messengers in which people talk about how the attitude of people around concerning their appearance affect them. They tell how and why they chose a particular way of expression in the context of appearance, as well as reflect on how hard it is to be different in our social environment. These monologues are the continuation of Tasha's publication 'Subjective Normality', which is dedicated to abandonment of imposed beauty standards in favour of individuality.

The event will be held in Russian.
