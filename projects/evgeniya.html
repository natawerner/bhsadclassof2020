light:metro:time — Evgeniya Velichko

The project is dedicated to pinhole photography. Using photography many unobserved things can be visualised. So, the project makes some hidden things obvious and represent the metro as a very interesting place where we can observe various things. The metro is always on the move, people are in a hurry and no one notices a lot of things happening around. Although people are used to perceiving the metro as a dark place, each metro station has a different light intensity. The final form of the project is a photo book with a collection of pictures taken using a pinhole camera. A pinhole camera is sensitive to light. A pinhole camera captures light intensity. And this light intensity is represented with transparent paper in the book. Turning the page, you can feel the interaction between light and time. Each page with a photo is placed under a certain number of transparent sheets. These numbers depend on the light intensity in the particular metro station.
