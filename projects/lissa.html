A Poetry of Perception — Lissa Lupicheva

A poetic text is the personal world of the author, where he expresses his ideas, thoughts, and emotions. The perception of this poetic world is very unique and personal for each person. The project shows different people reading the same poem out loud. Each participant of the project was asked to interpret and read the text in their own way, and none of them had any previous knowledge of either the poets’ intentions or any other background information connected with the poem. The different readers attempt to connect with the author through their text, and the sum of their voices constructs a new series of “collective” poems.
