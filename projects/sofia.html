And Other Stories — Sofa Kravtsova

The project "And Other Stories" is the first part of a book series that Sofa and her friend illustrator Ksenia were working on. Collectively they have created a whole world where fiction and fantasy, love and pain, adventures, and danger combine together.

Unlike other collaborative projects, this book has been written entirely online and demonstrates all the changes that the final text undergoes before it is finally finished. It shows all the grammar and spelling mistakes and along with that, it has notes from both authors, giving the reader a better understanding of a thought process. This book and the way it is written and designed invite a reader to a breathtaking journey around the world of imagination. Despite the stories not being connected at the first sight, they all share something. And it is up to the reader to discover what it is. And as we all know, creating something is a never-ending process. So is this book.
