Surface — Liza Falyugina

Concept of this project is to show that a tattoo can become as much a part of everyday life as any other part of appearance. Only the owner himself gives it meaning. Also, a tattoo does not affect the mental abilities of its owner. The decision on a tattoo is more likely to be made based on social connections, if not out of personal motives. This project is not about tattoos as an art, it is about people.
