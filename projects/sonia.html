The origins of electronic music — Sonia Solokhina

The project is dedicated to researching the themes of graphical sound and synthetic music created by Soviet inventors, engineers, physicists and composers, such as Evgeny Sholpo, Arseniy Avraamov, Boris Yankovsky, Nikolai Voinov, Evgeny Murzin and others.In the 30s they explored the field of ‘graphical sound’, exploring the relationship between man and machine, sound and image they sought to uncover the universal truths of geometry, art, film and music. It was their inventions and ideas that influenced the development of electronic music. This is a forgotten layer of history that is not much known about. This project, on the one hand, explores history through a documentary, which includes 4 parts, each part is dedicated to one instrument and its inventor and on the other hand, it is the embodiment of those methods that were invented earlier. This is a tribute to Soviet inventors, their ideas and inventions!
